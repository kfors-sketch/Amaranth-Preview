Rules & Tips
  • ID: lowercase letters, numbers, and dashes only (e.g. pre-reg, directory).
  • Type: fixed (one per person), qty (choose quantity), amount (open dollar amount), variantQty (choose from labeled variants).
  • Chair Emails: comma-separated. Leave blank if unknown.
  • Publish Start/End: local date/time (YYYY-MM-DDTHH:MM) for when the add-on is visible.
  • Limit/attendee: optional max a single attendee can purchase for this add-on.
