Rules & Tips
  • ID: lowercase letters, numbers, and dashes only (e.g. pa2026-banquet-chicken).
  • Chair Emails: comma-separated list; leave blank if unknown.
  • Meal Choices: leave blank for no choice, or comma-separated (e.g. chicken,beef,veg).
  • Publish Start/End: local date/time using the pickers below (YYYY-MM-DDTHH:MM).
  • Active: controls visibility; toggle to retire/restore.
