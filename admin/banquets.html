Rules & Tips
  • ID: lowercase letters, numbers, and dashes only (e.g. trails-feast).
  • Chair Emails: comma-separated list; leave blank if unknown.
  • Meal Choices: leave blank for no choice, or comma-separated (e.g. chicken,beef,veg).
  • Event Date/Time: choose the local date/time of the banquet.
  • Publish Start/End: local date/time (YYYY-MM-DDTHH:MM) for when the product is live/closed.
  • Report Frequency: controls how often chair reports are sent for this banquet.
